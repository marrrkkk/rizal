## Animation Timing

- **Entrance**: 800ms ease-out
- **Display**: 5000ms (5 seconds)
- **Exit**: 500ms ease-in
- **Stagger**: 500ms between multiple notifications
- **Icon Pulse**: Continuous during display
- **Sparkles**: Ping animation with delays (100ms, 200ms, 300ms)

---

## Usage Tips

1. **Single Achievement**: Shows centered, full drama
2. **Multiple Achievements**: Staggered by 500ms each
3. **Legendary Achievements**: Extra sparkle effects automatically added
4. **Click to Dismiss**: Users can click "Awesome!" button
5. **Auto-Dismiss**: Automatically closes after 5 seconds

---

## Accessibility

- High contrast text on dark backgrounds
- Large, clear icons
- Readable font sizes
- Smooth, non-jarring animations
- Keyboard accessible (can dismiss with Enter/Space)
- Screen reader friendly with proper ARIA labels

---
